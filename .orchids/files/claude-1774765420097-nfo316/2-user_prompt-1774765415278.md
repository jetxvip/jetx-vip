JetX.vp — Copy Update + Scroll Indicator (Sitewide)

There are two required changes across the site.

## 1. Remove any fixed response-time promise

I do NOT want the site to promise any exact callback/response time such as:

* "Response within 2 hours"
* "A representative will get back within 2 hours"
* or any other time-based guarantee

This kind of wording creates unnecessary legal and business risk.

### Required replacement

Everywhere on the site where the copy currently implies a fixed response time, replace it with messaging that only communicates urgency and responsiveness, without committing to a specific time.

Use wording in the same language/context of the page such as:

**Hebrew:**

* נציג יחזור אליך במהירות המרבית
* נחזור אליך בהקדם האפשרי
* הצוות שלנו יחזור אליך במהירות המרבית

**English:**

* A representative will get back to you as quickly as possible
* Our team will respond as quickly as possible
* We will get back to you at the earliest opportunity

### Important

* Apply this sitewide
* Find ALL occurrences of any time-based promise
* Replace them consistently
* Do not leave any “2 hours” / “within X hours” wording anywhere

---

## 2. Add a right-side animated scroll indicator across the site

I want a visual cue that helps users understand the page continues below and that they should scroll down.

### Requirement

Add an animated scroll indicator on the **right side of the screen** across the site.

### Behavior

* visible on page load
* clearly suggests that more content is available below
* should visually indicate both:

  * that scrolling is possible
  * roughly how much more page remains

### Design direction

* elegant / premium / minimal
* consistent with JetX visual language
* subtle animation
* should not feel cheap or distracting

### Recommended behavior

* fixed on the right side
* small vertical progress-style indicator or scroll cue
* updates as the user scrolls
* fades or becomes less prominent when near the bottom
* should work smoothly on desktop and mobile, unless mobile behavior needs to be simplified for UX

### Important

* must not block content
* must not interfere with buttons or forms
* must not hurt performance
* must be responsive and visually refined

---

## Final requirement

Please implement both changes cleanly and consistently across the full site without breaking layout, responsiveness, or existing functionality.
