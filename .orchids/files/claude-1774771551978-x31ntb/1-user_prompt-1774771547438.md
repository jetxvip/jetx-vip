JetX.vp — Sold Out Overlay Final Refinement

The current Sold Out direction is visually correct and much closer to what I want.

However, two refinements are still required:

## 1. The Sold Out text must be sharper

The text on the diagonal red band is currently too faded / blurred.

### Requirement:

* keep the diagonal red band
* keep the dramatic premium look
* but make the text itself much sharper and easier to read

The text must be:

* fully opaque
* high contrast
* crisp
* immediately readable

Do NOT let the text inherit too much transparency from the overlay.

---

## 2. The flight/offer card underneath is too washed out

Right now, the entire card becomes too faded / too blurred when marked as Sold Out.

I do want the card to look unavailable,
but I still want users to clearly see the offer underneath.

### Requirement:

* reduce the amount of fade / washout / blur on the card
* keep the sold-out feeling
* but preserve more visibility of:

  * route
  * aircraft
  * date
  * price
  * overall card content

The card should still feel premium and readable, not overly muted.

---

## Desired final balance

I want:

* a strong red diagonal Sold Out band
* sharp readable Sold Out text
* slightly muted background card
* but NOT excessively faded

So the design should communicate:

* this offer is gone
* but the offer still looks real, attractive, and visible

---

## Important

Do not remove the current dramatic diagonal treatment.
Only refine it by:

* increasing text clarity
* decreasing the amount of card washout underneath

---

## Expected result

* same dramatic diagonal sold-out effect
* clearer Sold Out / אזל המלאי text
* less washed-out card underneath
* better overall visual balance
