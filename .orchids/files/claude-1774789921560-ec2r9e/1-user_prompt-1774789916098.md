That means the Ambulance Flights page itself is broken at code/runtime level and must be fixed before production can be considered ready.

## Current problem

When navigating to the Ambulance Flights page:

* the page turns white
* the route crashes
* sometimes navigation back also results in a white screen

This must be treated as a blocking issue.

Investigate the Ambulance Flights page and all components/data it depends on.

* invalid imports
